Our Departments

Cardiology

Medistar Heart Institutes are very well known for performing a multitude of treatments and procedures in cardiology and cardiothoracic surgery. The scorecard shows a formidable record cardiac and cardiothoracic surgeries.

We have vast experience in the most complicated coronary artery bypass surgery, surgery for all types of valvular heart diseases, paediatric heart surgery, adult and paediatric heart transplantation with success rates comparable to international standards.

Nephrology

Nephrology is a branch of medical science that deals with the diagnosis and treatment of disorders related to the kidney. Nephrology is a centre of excellence at Medistar and sees more than 10,000 patients every year.

Medistar houses some of the very best specialists in the world who specialize in management of kidney failure, dialysis, diabetic nephropathy, to kidney transplants. Medistar Hospitals is your one-stop destination to book the best Nephrologists in your town.

Neurology

Neurology is a centre of excellence at Medistar and deals with diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the nervous system. The Medistar Neuro departments are equipped with the best team and state of the art facilities like Neuro radiology, sleep lab, Intensive care units etc.

Our Neuro team takes care of more than 300 variety of problems like aneurisms, dementia, Alzheimer's, epilepsy, cerebro vascular diseases, headache, stroke, sleep neurology. Medistar Hospitals is your one-stop destination to book the best Nephrologists in your town.

Oncology

Cancer is a ruthless disease. It evolves constantly and spreads relentlessly. It's hard to catch and even harder to treat.

But that is about to change, with Precision Oncology. Medistar's unique approach to diagnosing, treating and monitoring cancer that is as precise as it can get. Cancer is Conquerable. And you can conquer it too.

Pulmonary

Pulmonary Medicine at Medistar Hospitals provides expertise in a broad range of diseases including asthma, COPD, critical care, cystic fibrosis, interstitial lung disease, lung cancer, neuromuscular disease, pulmonary hypertension, sarcoidosis, and sleep medicine.

Our smoke cessation programs are the best in the country with huge success rate and are implemented by many corporate companies for their employees.

Orthopedics

The Medistar Institutes of Orthopaedics is regarded as one of the best & top orthopaedics hospitals in India with a legacy of innovation and excellence.

Medistar Hospitals offers key & best orthopedic surgeries and procedures in India. We perform bone and joint replacement surgeries which include the most current Arthroscopic and Reconstructive techniques.
